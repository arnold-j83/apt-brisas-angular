Keeping cool in summer

Spanish architects and builders are no strangers to the hot summer sun. The apartment is designed with cavity walls and lots of windows each with fully fitting roller shutters. Even at the hottest times the apartment will stay cool with natural ventilation through windows and doors and use of the ceiling fan. During the day the rooms will remain comfortably cool with the shutters down. It is at night, when the structure gives off heat that has built up during the day that you may feel the heat.

Keeping warm in winter

The Spanish Mediterranean coast is generally warm and sunny even in winter. However though the days are warm and sunny the night can get quite chilly. The apartment is equipped with two heaters that will quickly warm the rooms as required.
